# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Local developers who need to manage, route, and proxy their models from Codex or OpenCode locally.

## Product Purpose
A clean dashboard to manage model configurations, active providers (like OpenRouter or OpenAI pool), and monitor proxy status efficiently without manual config file edits.

## Brand Personality
Clean, minimalist, SaaS-like, professional, and precise.

## Anti-references
Bubbly SaaS, overly spaced marketing templates, generic "dashboard" themes with excessive padding or playful/childish elements.

## Design Principles
- **Function over decoration**: Every element on screen must serve a clear purpose for configuration or monitoring.
- **High signal-to-noise**: Data (models, endpoints, statuses) should be easy to scan and act upon.
- **Predictable control**: Toggles, forms, and settings should feel stable, immediate, and native.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard A11y: Keyboard navigable, standard contrast ratios, and screen-reader accessible forms and statuses.
